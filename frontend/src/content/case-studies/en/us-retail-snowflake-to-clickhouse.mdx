## The Problem

A US retail client was drowning in Snowflake costs — **$60K/month and climbing**. The root cause: 1,200 Workato recipes running full-refresh ETL against Snowflake, plus 124 stored procedures with no owner. Nobody knew what half of them did.

<CostMeter client:visible />

## Orchestration Overhaul

Led architecture decisions across the full rebuild. Replaced the entire Workato layer with Airflow and eliminated full-refresh ETL via CDC.

<PipelineCollapse client:visible />

Collapsed **1,200 Workato recipes + 124 Snowflake stored procedures** into 10 Airflow DAGs (~50 tasks each). Every pipeline became version-controlled, observable, and maintainable.

## Warehouse Migration

Rebuilt the analytics layer as a **medallion lakehouse on S3 + ClickHouse**:

<MedallionFlow client:visible />

- **ClickPipes** ingestion into ClickHouse
- **ReplacingMergeTree** tables + materialized views for dedup and incremental aggregation
- **dbt-managed** transformations and schema migrations
- **Projections** for query acceleration

## Migration Tooling

Migrated ~1,350 data objects in ~5 days using custom **Python + Gemini/Claude** tooling for:
- Snowflake → ClickHouse SQL dialect translation
- DDL generation from Snowflake metadata
- Automated error debugging and retry

## Results

- **$60K/month** eliminated from the client's data stack
- **1,200 → 10** orchestration pipelines, fully observable
- **124 stored procedures → dbt models** with lineage tracking
- **~1,350 objects migrated in ~5 days** using LLM-assisted tooling
- Mentored the client's Workato team through the Airflow transition
